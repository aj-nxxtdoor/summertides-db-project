SummerTides Festival Database Presentation
Project Overview
Built a relational database for a multi-day beach festival.
Modeled attendees, tickets, performances, vendors, sales, sponsors, and stages.
Added sample data and reusable SQL views for reporting.
Business Value
Reduces duplicate records and spreadsheet chaos.
Supports ticketing, artist scheduling, vendor reporting, and sponsor tracking.
Enables simple business queries for festival operations.
Technical Highlights
SQLite-compatible schema with foreign keys and integrity checks.
Seed data for 20 attendees, 15 artists, 6 stages, 30 performances, 40 tickets, 10 vendors, 50 sales, and 8 sponsors.
Query scripts for selection, filtering, grouping, joins, views, and bonus analytics.
